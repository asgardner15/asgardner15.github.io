Introduction

Welcome to a beginner-friendly guide to American Mahjongg. This site is designed to make the game feel less overwhelming by explaining the basics in a simple, clear way.

What is American Mahjongg?

American Mahjongg is a tile-based fame played with a group of players using tiles, racks/pushers, and the National Mahjongg League playing card. The goal is to be the first player to get a winning combination of 14 tiles that matches any one of the lines on the NJML card.

Why People Enjoy Playing

American Mahjongg is fun because it combines strategy. memory, decision-making, and social interaction. It can seem confusing at first, but once the basics make sense, the game becomes much easier to follow.

Basic Supplies Needed

  1. A set of Mahjongg tiles
  2. Four racks and pushers
  3. A pair of dice
  4. The current National Mah Jongg League card
  5. A table to play on

Recommended Supplies

  1. A playing mat
  2. A tile storage bag

Quick Tip

Don't try to memorize everything at once! Start by learning the tile types and how to read the card. The rest of hte game becomes easier with practice!
